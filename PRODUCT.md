# Product

## The problem

Private clinics (dental, GP, aesthetics/beauty) run on a single front-desk
team that also has to answer the phone — and during peak hours (opening,
lunch, closing) that team is simply overloaded. The result, backed by
industry research:

- **25–35% of inbound calls to a clinic go unanswered.**
- Missed calls translate directly into missed bookings — clinics lose an
  estimated **€6,000–8,000 per month** in revenue from appointments that
  were never made because the call wasn't picked up.

Clinics can't easily solve this by hiring more front-desk staff — call
volume peaks are short and unpredictable, and a receptionist on the line
24/7 isn't realistic for a single-location private practice.

## The solution

Aimée is an AI voice agent that answers the clinic's phone line directly.
It talks to callers in natural language, in the clinic's own language, and
handles the three things that matter most for a clinic's calendar:

- **Booking** a new appointment
- **Rescheduling** an existing appointment
- **Cancelling** an appointment

It reads and writes directly to the clinic's existing calendar/CRM, so staff
keep using the tools they already have — Aimée just fills the gaps a human
receptionist can't cover around the clock.

## Who it's for

Private clinics in the UK, France, the US and Russia — primarily dental
practices, GP/aesthetic clinics and beauty salons — that run on a booking
calendar and feel the peak-hour front-desk squeeze described above.

## What makes it different

Most voice-agent demos sound like a robot reading a script. Aimée is built
to sound like a good receptionist: **short, warm, and to the point.** It
doesn't over-explain, it doesn't ask for information it doesn't need, and it
gets the caller to a booked (or rescheduled, or cancelled) appointment in as
few turns as possible.

## Business model

Subscription SaaS, billed monthly per clinic, with a usage-based component
(included call minutes per plan, metered overage beyond that). A free trial
lets a clinic test the agent on real calls before committing.
